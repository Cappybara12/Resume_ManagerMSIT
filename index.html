Frequently Asked Questions

Clear Your Doubts!

1. What should I do if I need assistance during the placement process?

To register with the placement cell, navigate to the "Student Registration" section on our website and fill out the registration form with your details, including academic information, skills, and resume upload.

2. How can I register with the placement cell?

To register with the placement cell, navigate to the "Student Registration" section on our website and fill out the registration form with your details, including academic information, skills, and resume upload.

3. How do I track the status of my job applications?

You can track the status of your job applications in the "Application Tracking" section of your profile. We regularly update the status of your applications as per the recruitment process.

4. Can I access past placement statistics?

Yes, you can view past placement statistics, including placement drives, companies, and job roles offered, in the "Past Placements" section.

5. How do companies shortlist candidates for interviews?

Companies use various criteria to shortlist candidates, including academic performance, skills, and relevant experience. Each company may have specific requirements for their roles.
